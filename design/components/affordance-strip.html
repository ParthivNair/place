Usage

  • The verb carries the only 600 weight; every other term stays 15px ink-soft. Hierarchy through type, not chrome.
  • Dog / kid flags are first-class Affordance attributes — 60.6% of Portland households own a pet — never an edge case.
  • Durations and distances are quantities → monospace .data; difficulty words are claim attributes → plain UI type.
  • The hazard-gated chip never renders without provenance and assumption-of-risk framing (docs/02 §5).
  • Drive time is not part of this strip — it lives in the card header.
